HONNE

Honne (stylised as HONNE) is an English electronic music duo formed in 2014 in Bow, London, consisting of James Hatcher (producer) and Andy Clutterbuck (singer, producer).

The band released their first two EPs, Warm on a Cold Night and All in the Value, on Super Recordings in 2014.
In 2015 they released their third EP, Coastal Love, on their own Atlantic Records imprint, Tatemae Recordings.
Honne's debut studio album, Warm on a Cold Night (Tatemae/Atlantic), was released 22 July 2016; their second, Love Me/Love Me Not (Tatemae/Atlantic), followed on 28 August 2018.
